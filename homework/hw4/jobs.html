Work at JavaJam

Barista

Are you passionate about crafting the perfect cup of coffee? Our coffee shop is looking for skilled baristas to join our team. As a barista, you will be the heart of our operation, responsible for preparing and serving exceptional coffee beverages and providing outstanding customer service. Your tasks will include:

  • Brewing and preparing a variety of coffee and espressso drinks.
  • Maintaining cleanliness and organization in the coffee preparation area.
  • Engaging with customers, taking orders, and providing a welcoming atmosphere.
  • Managing inventory and restocking supplies as needed.

  • Hourly wage: $12/hr + tips

Cashier

If you're a friendly and detail-oriented individual, we have an opening for a cashier at our coffee shop. As a cashier, you will be the first point of contact for our customers and play a crucial role in ensuring a smooth and efficient ordering process. Your tasks will include:

  • Greeting customers with a warm and welcoming demeanor.
  • Accurately processing orders and handling cash or electronic payments.
  • Providing information about our menu items and promotions.
  • Maintaining a tidy and organized cash register area.

  • Hourly wage: $10/hr + tips

Kitchen Assistant

Behind every great coffee shop, there's a dedicated kitchen assistant. We're looking for team memebers who can hlep us prepare delicious pastries and snacks to complement our beverages. Your tasks will include:

  • Assisting in the preparation and baking of pastries, sandwiches, and snacks.
  • Ensuring the kitchen area is clean and well-organized.
  • Monitoring inventory and restocking ingredients as needed.
  • Collaborating with the chef to maintain quality and consistency.

  • Hourly wage: $13/hr

Shift Supervisor

If you have experience in the food service industry and a knack for leadership, consider joining us as a shift supervisor. In this role, you'll oversee the daily operations of the coffee shop, ensuring that everything runs smoothly and efficiently. Your tasks will include:

  • Supervising and guiding the baristas and kitchen staff during your shift.
  • Ensuring exceptional customer service and resolving any customer concerns.
  • Managing inventory, ordering supplies, and tracking expenses.
  • Opening or closing the shop as per the schedule.

  • Hourly wage: $15/hr


If you're interested in any of these positions and want to be part of our coffee shop team, please submit your resume and a brief cover letter highlighting your relevant experience. We look forward to meeting you!
